    An english woman in NY:

    The plane arrives in Los Angeles at 2 p.m. With the three-hour time difference, it is 3 p.m. in New York when it is noon in Los Angeles. The plane arrives in New York at 8 p.m., so the outbound flight took five hours. The return flight to Los Angeles also lasts five hours, which means that the plane lands at 5 p.m. New York time. Taking into account the time difference, it can be concluded that the plane arrives in Los Angeles at 14:00.


    Some quick maths:

    You have to guess how long a mathematician has been around based on his or her statements, which you can turn into an equation. The x value corresponds to the mathematician's lifetime. With the first sentence, we deduce that x/6 + x/12 + x/7. Then 5 + x/2 + 4. The equation gives: x = x (1/6 + 1/12 + 1/7 + 1/2) + 5 + 4. With a common denominator: x = x (14/84 + 7/84 + 12/84 + 42/84) + 9. We then see that x = x (75/84) + 9 = 75x/84 + 9. x - 75x/84 = 9. Then, by reducing we have 9x/84 = 9 so x = 84. So the man died at 84.

    A commercial magician:

    The answer is 211 beads. The number of beads the traveller brought back (minus one) is a whole number between 1 and 500, divisible by 2,3,5,6 and 7. To find this number, list the multiples of 7 less than 500 and divide them by other numbers. Any number divisible by 6 is also divisible by 2 and 3, so just find a number divisible by 6 and 5. This leaves only 210, and you only need to add one to find the answer. The magician, on the other hand, is waiting for 421 beads.


    A mysterious number:

    The question mark should be replaced by the number 89! In fact, you have to find the square of each of the digits of a number and then add them together. This will give you the next number in the sequence.
